========================================
IF YOU GET STUCK:
========================================

Problem: Railway login not working
Solution: Make sure you have a Railway account at railway.app

Problem: "Cannot connect to backend"
Solution: Check that NEXT_PUBLIC_API_URL is set in Vercel

Problem: Vercel shows error
Solution: Wait 2-3 minutes for deployment to finish
